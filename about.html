About Us

Trusted local plumbers serving Coffs Harbour and the Mid North Coast

Our Story

Elite Plumb & Gas was built on a simple idea: provide honest, professional plumbing and gas fitting services to the people of Coffs Harbour and the Mid North Coast at fair prices.

Zach and the team bring years of hands-on experience and a genuine commitment to quality workmanship. We're not just here to fix your plumbing—we're here to build lasting relationships with our customers based on trust, reliability, and results.

Whether it's a leaky tap, a gas installation, or a major plumbing overhaul, we approach every job with the same level of professionalism and attention to detail. We take pride in showing up on time, doing the work right, and leaving you with a smile.
